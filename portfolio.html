About Me

I am a web developer by day and esports manager by night. My skills acquired through the years as a designer have taught me that the ideal way to desing anyone's site is to create a positive user experience while also fullfilling the needs of the client. I've come to work on multiple projects including the maintenance of websites through The Space Dynamics Laboratory, managaing and overseeing the creation of the Beachcon website along with it's revamp for 2019, and fulfilling the task of creating a whole new site from the ground up for the University Nanosatellite Program.

Through the Space Dynamics Lab I was able to understand and work in an agile work environment to meet client deadlines and have since implemented it in my work with Beachcon and through other projects I have made or designed alongside friends. With this design philosophy we have been able to implement the designs we've put together using hand drawn wire frames and guidance from our clients, project managers, and graphic designers
